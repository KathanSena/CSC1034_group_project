About the charity

We work with local volunteers, schools, and community groups to distribute food support with dignity. Our aim is to make the process simple, local, and dependable.

What we provide

Food parcels

Short-term support for households facing immediate hardship.

Pantry collections

Low-cost and free food collection points in local community spaces.

Referrals

Signposting to other support services when extra help is needed.

Current impact

18 community hubs
420 households supported weekly
32 active volunteers
